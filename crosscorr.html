crosscorr - Analysis of Kerr-gate cross-correlation data¶

A collection of functions to process and analyze Kerr-gate cross-correlation data. Data are analyzed by fitting each spectral slice of the cross-correlation with a Gaussian pulse shape to extract chirp, temporal offset and FWHM.

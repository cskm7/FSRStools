yeh_tm.System.get_electric_field¶

System.get_electric_field(w, dz, pol=(1, 0), x=0.0, y=0.0)¶

Calculate the electric field profile for a given frequency.

The z-axis range is taken according to the total thickness of the layer stack plus the thicknesses of substrate and superstrate.

Parameters:
  • w (float) – Frequency value.
  • dz (float) – Step size along the z-axis.
  • pol (tuple) – (Complex) Ratio of x- and y-amplitudes of incident radiation (default = x-pol).
  • x (float) – x-coordinate.
  • y (float) – y-coordinate.
Returns:

z-axis, complex electric field vector (3d) along z-axis ([z][Ex, Ey, Ez]), and list of z-coordinates of interfaces (zn).

Important

This function ignores the thick parameter, i.e. propagation is always coherent.

fitting.fit_waist¶

fitting.fit_waist(y, dz)¶

Convenience function for extracting the beam waist from a knife-edge intensity profile using waist() and regularly spaced points.

Parameters:
  • y (array) – Transmitted power.
  • dz (float) – Change in z-position of knife edge between subsequent power readings.
Returns:

Fit results

  • A amplitude, total power,
  • y0 offset, background light level,
  • z0 center of beam,
  • w0 beam waist defined as - radius of intensity.

rraman.prefF¶

rraman.prefF(eEF, M, IOR, dt)¶

Return the prefactor for the fluorescence efficiency calculation (unitless). See getCrossSections() for more details.

Parameters:
  • eEF (array) – Fluorescence energy in (cm-1). May also be a single float value.
  • M (float) – Electronic transition dipole length in (A).
  • IOR (float) – Index of refraction of surrounding solvent / medium.
  • dt (float) – Time increment used for integration (fs).
Returns:

Prefactor for fluorescence efficiency calculation.

See also

Myers, Chem. Phys. 180, 215 (1994), Eqs. (6) and (26).

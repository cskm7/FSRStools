rraman.prefR¶

rraman.prefR(eEL, M, eR, dt)¶

Return the prefactor for the Raman excitation profile calculation (A**2 / molec).

Parameters:
  • eEL (array) – Laser excitation energies in (cm-1). Can also be a single floating point value.
  • M (float) – Electronic transition dipole moment in (A).
  • eR (float) – Stokes shift of the Raman line in (cm-1).
  • dt (float) – Time increment for the integration (fs).
Returns:

The prefactor for the Raman excitation profile calculation.

See also

Myers, Eq. (34) and following text.

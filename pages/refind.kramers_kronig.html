refind.kramers_kronig¶

refind.kramers_kronig(w, k)¶

Calculate the Kramers-Kronig transformation of absorption spectrum k as function of angular frequency w. This formulation is to be used for the refractive index, not for the permittivity!

Parameters:
  • w (array) – Angular frequency axis.
  • k (array) – Absorption spectrum (same shape as w).
Returns:

Real part of refractive index as obtained by Kramers-Kronig transformation.

Important

k and w have to be sampled on a uniform grid!!!

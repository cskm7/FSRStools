rraman.getCrossSections¶

rraman.getCrossSections(wn, dt, tdpart, ovlps, sshift, M, IOR, sig=0, ialpha=1)¶

Calculate the absorption and Raman cross-sections.

Parameters:
  • wn (array) – Wavenumber axis in (cm-1).
  • dt (float) – Time increment for integration / tdpart.
  • tdpart (array) – Zero-zero energy and damping terms in the time domain, same shape as wn.
  • ovlps (array) – M + 1 Absorption and Raman overlap integrals.
  • sshift (float) – Vibrational freqencies of M Raman modes to calculate (cm-1).
  • IOR (float) – Index of refraction of surrounding medium / solvent.
  • sig (float) – Linewidth for inhomogeneous damping (standard deviation of Gaussian), set to zero if not used (default).
  • ialpha (float) –

    Lineshape parameter for inhomogeneous damping:

    • 1 = Gaussian (default),
    • 0 = Lorentzian.
Returns:

Absorption and M Raman cross sections (arrays with same shape as wn): sigmaA, sigmaR[M].

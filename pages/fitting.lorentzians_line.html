fitting.lorentzians_line¶

fitting.lorentzians_line(x, a, b, *p)¶

Sum over variable number of Lorentzians on a linear background.

Parameters:
  • x (array) – x-values.
  • a (float) – y-intercept.
  • b (float) – Slope.
  • p (variable) –

    Coefficients for Lorentzians. For each Lorentzian provide

    • A amplitude,
    • x0 center,
    • dx FWHM.
Returns:

Sum over Lorentzians .

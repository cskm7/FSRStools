fitting.gaussians_quartic¶

fitting.gaussians_quartic(x, a, b, c, d, e, *p)¶

Sum over a variable number of Gaussians on a quartic background.

Parameters:
  • x (array) – x-values.
  • a (float) – Offset.
  • b (float) – Linear term.
  • c (float) – Quadratic term.
  • d (float) – Cubic term.
  • e (float) – Quartic term.
  • p (variable) –

    Coefficients for Gaussians. For each Gaussian provide

    • A amplitude,
    • x0 center,
    • dx FWHM.
Returns:

Sum over Gaussians .

See also

gaussians() for details.

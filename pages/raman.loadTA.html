raman.loadTA¶

raman.loadTA(basename, wl=[], timesteps=[], excstr='*', filteroutliers=False)¶

Convenience function for loading transient absorption data. The only difference to loadFSRS() is the naming convention for TA excited state data compared to FSRS data.

See also

loadFSRS() for list of parameters.
